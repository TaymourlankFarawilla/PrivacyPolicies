Privacy Policy for PuzzleTide2026

Effective date: March 26, 2026

PuzzleTide is a mobile sliding puzzle game published by Taymourlank Farawilla. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when you use the app.

Summary

  • The app stores game progress and selected images locally on your device.
  • The app shows ads using Google Mobile Ads (AdMob), which may collect device and usage data.
  • The app does not upload your photos or game data to our servers.

Information We Collect

1) Ads and Analytics (Google Mobile Ads)

The app uses Google Mobile Ads to display banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads. Google may collect:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
  • IP address (may be used for approximate location)
  • App usage and ad interaction data
  • Crash and performance data related to ads

This data is processed by Google in accordance with their policies. We do not receive access to your raw personal data.

2) Local Game Data (Stored On Device)

  • Best moves and best time per level
  • Total wins
  • Image selections for image puzzles

3) Photos (Only When You Choose One)

When you pick an image for the puzzle, the app accesses your photo library only to select that image. The chosen image is copied into app storage and is not uploaded.

How We Use Information

  • Show ads and provide rewarded hints
  • Track your local progress and best scores
  • Store selected images for image puzzles

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data. Data used for ads is handled by Google under their policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Ads Data Policies

Permissions

  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES (Android 13+): to let you select images for puzzles
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and below): to let you select images for puzzles
  • INTERNET: to load ads

Your Choices

  • You can reset your Advertising ID in your device settings.
  • You can opt out of personalized ads in your device or Google settings.
  • You can delete stored images by using the in-app image library.

Children's Privacy

PuzzleTide is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are significant, we will update the effective date.
